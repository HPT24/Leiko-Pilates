Reformer and Apparatus Pilates

Reformer Pilates is a fantastic way to exercise, especially for lower impact with targeted effects, and is a wonderful supplement to mat based practices as well as for rehabilitation from injury.

Apparatus Pilates offers a broader range of equipment beyond the Reformer, such as the Cadillac, Combo Chair, and Ladder Barrel. Each piece of equipment offers unique exercises and challenges, contributing to a more diverse workout.

Ladder Barrel

Helps to achieve supported positions for deep stretching to elongate the body. From beginners to the well-versed exerciser, the Ladder Barrel challenges core stability, spinal strength and overall flexibility

Combo Chair

Offers several benefits, including improving strength, balance, flexibility, and overall body awareness. It also helps stability through controlled movements and allows for a wide range of exercises for both beginner and advanced people

Cadillac

Cadillac resembles a bed frame in the design, with four posts, a raised platform, and an overhead canopy-like component called a trapeze. It incorporates springs, handles, and bars that escalate the user experience

Reformer

Designed to improve strength and boost flexibility, balance, posture and coordination. The reformer is a bed-like machine that moves back and forward and uses springs to increase resistance as you workout
